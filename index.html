Bio

I'm currently working toward the collaborative Ph.D. degree between Shanghai University supervised by Prof. Ping An and the University of Technology Sydney supervised by A/Prof. Qiang Wu. Before that, I received my B.E. degree, in 2018, from the School of Communication and Information Engineering, Shanghai University, Shanghai, China.

My research interests include light field/multi-view image processing.
